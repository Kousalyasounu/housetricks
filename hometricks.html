#Who And What We Are:

House Tricks, an architecture firm based in Andhra, was founded in 2018 by Lakshmi and Karthik, both graduates of the Andhra University Architecture program. House Tricks is derived from the marriage of context and architecture which is what drives the design philosophy of the studio. In its inception the firm focused primarily on residential architecture, but as the firm grew it diversified its body of work to include multifamily residential, restaurant and coffee shops, manufacturing, and senior care facilities. Large or small, the design philosophy is carried through each of the projects. The firm's exclusive use of BIM helps with early visualization, better consultant coordination, more timely delivery of the projects and more consistent work from project to project
